// about

Teaching by doing.

Led primarily by undergraduate students, we organize workshops, journal clubs, guest speaker events, and collaborative projects to build skills and explore careers in this interdisciplinary field.

// our mission

To foster a community that supplements the education of those curious about computational neuroscience through interactive workshops, engaging journal clubs, and collaborative projects. By providing these opportunities, we aim to increase the confidence of our members in the field, preparing them for future endeavors while giving space to discuss interests in the field.

// what is computational neuroscience?

The study of the brain's function using mathematical models, computer simulations, and other computational techniques. It's a multidisciplinary field that combines neuroscience with computer science, mathematics, and other disciplines.
