One idea, many campuses.

CompNeuroSociety started at FSU - and it's growing. We've partnered with students at Florida International University (FIU) to launch a new CompNeuroSociety chapter, so more students can learn computational neuroscience by doing real research together.

// FSU × FIU

Our first partner chapter is at FIU, run by FIU students with mentorship from our network. Project teams, workshops, and journal clubs run in the same spirit as at FSU - free, hands-on, and open to every major.
